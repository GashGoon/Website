Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I put my hands up?

Press X on your Keyboard

How do I talk in-game?

You hold N or go to settings and change the keybind

How do I respawn?

You can either do /revive to revive in that spot or do /respawn to respawn at the hospital

How do I delete a vehicle?

You type /dv in chat

How do I get staff?

You have to work your way through the ranks and obtain the staff rank
